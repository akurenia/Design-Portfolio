NEDC: Stories From Experience

NEDC has produced a learning resource for adults who are recovering from eating disorders called Stories From Experience - the brief was to deliver Stories From Experience digitally in an engaging manner.

User Interface, User Experience

The website does not serve only as an online version of the existing modules, but also as a learning tool that assists the recovery. With this site, users can send their thoughts privately to their loved ones & also bookmark readings that resonates with them.

When user landed on their dashboard, they are presented first and foremost with their learning progress with the option to continue with their current reading. The navigation allows them to jump between modules seamlessly anywhere on the site.

A playful but simple interface is the key here to get users encouraged to finish the modules & grasp the content easily. I was given the opportunity to lead the visual direction of the site & had so much fun illustrating throughout the website.

Adding to that, the website needs to be responsive so that it is accessible anywhere & anytime.
