A bit more about me

Hello there. I'm Aurenia, a UI/UX Designer and a part time illustrator. Indonesian born and raised, currently working at Bliss Media, Melbourne based digital agency.

My journey in UX design begins with my curiosity in people & my passion in learning. Whilst studying & working in Australia, I learned the importance of being exposed to different perspectives & having my ideas challenged. I realised that I’ve always been inspired by stories and people. With UX Design, I’m able to touch on different kind of subjects & fulfil my passion in learning.

My strength lies in User Interface Design as well as delivering well-thought experience with end-user in mind. Outside of design, I spend time travelling, learning how to code, listening to podcasts, and taking photos.

Have a flick at my portfolio and feel free to contact me if you'd like to have a chat.
