A bit more about me

Hello there. I'm Aurenia, a UI/UX Designer and a part time illustrator. Indonesian born and raised, but currently based in Melbourne, Australia.

Since I was very young I've always been interested in learning and trying new things. Other than illustration, I was fairly good at Math and taught myself HTML/CSS back in highschool. At some point I realised that my passion lies in learning and being creative. In late 2012, I moved to Australia to pursue my BA of Communication Design degree at RMIT University.

Though I've always had a great appreciation in beautiful interfaces (coming from a traditional graphic design background), I want my design to be impactful. With an ever evolving field like UX, I'm able to touch on wide variety of subjects and fulfill my passion in learning and creating.

Outside of design, I spend time travelling with friends, making cheesy birthday cards, listening to podcasts, and taking photos.

Have a flick at my portfolio and feel free to contact me if you'd like to have a chat.
