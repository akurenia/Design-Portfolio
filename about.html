A bit more about me

Hello there. I'm Aurenia, a UI/UX Designer, an illustrator, and an occassional bathroom singer. Indonesian born and raised, currently work at Bliss Media, Melbourne based digital agency.

My journey in UX design begins with my curiosity in people. Whilst studying & working in Australia, I realised I’ve always been inspired by stories & how important it is to be exposed to different perspectives. I also have a fondness on beautiful things. With UX Design, I’m able to touch on different kind of subjects & fulfil my passion in learning.

My strength lies in User Interface Design as well as delivering well-thought experience with end-user in mind. Outside of design, I spend time travelling, learning how to code, listening to podcasts, and taking photos.

Have a flick at my portfolio and feel free to contact me if you'd like to have a chat.
